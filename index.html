BIGGER

BIGGER is a game based on 2048 and Threes, produced by Figgy Games. When two circle tiles of the same size touch, they merge into one and become BIGGER!
  • Use your finger to swipe tiles on the screen
  • Combine tiles of the same size to make bigger tiles
  • Keep combining until you reach the biggest tile!
    • Features:
  • No understanding of numbers or math necessary
  • Made for people of all ages
  • Recommended for toddlers and children


    FUNHOUSE FISHBOWL

    Funhouse Fishbowl is a fishbowl simulation game produced by Figgy Games. Move your fish by tapping or swiping your screen.
  • Use your finger to tap and place your fish in a new area
  • Swipe the screen to make your fish move
    • Features:
  • Made for people of all ages
  • Recommended for children, adults, and animals
